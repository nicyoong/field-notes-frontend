About

Field Notes is a portfolio-ready “research team publishing externally” site: dense layouts, multi-level hierarchy, and readability-first interaction design.

Mission make thinking legible Method claims + evidence Output essays, briefs, reports

Mission

Publish research as an artifact of organizational clarity. Writing forces decisions into explicit claims, connects them to evidence, and makes tradeoffs discussable.

Working principle
“If it can’t be explained, it can’t be evaluated.” Use this site to show your ability to build navigation systems that support thinking, not just reading.

Methodology

Each piece should disclose:

  • Scope and data sources
  • Confidence level on key claims
  • Open questions + next research steps

Tip: keep a consistent taxonomy (topics) so Insights filtering stays meaningful.

Use this for speaking, advisory work, research partnerships, or consulting inquiries.
